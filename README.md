# ERNST SCHRÖDER

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

ERNST SCHRÖDER (1841–1902) — LOGIKĒ, the BOOLEAN CLEANUP. By the 1880s the algebra of logic was a brilliant mess (Boole started it, Jevons tidied it, Peirce deepened it with relations) scattered across clashing papers. Schröder built the CATHEDRAL: the three-volume VORLESUNGEN ÜBER DIE ALGEBRA DER LOGIK (1890–1905) gathered it all into one rigorous structure — the canonical laws of MEET (∧), JOIN (∨), and COMPLEMENT, the principle of DUALITY (swap ∧↔∨ and 0↔1 and every theorem still holds), recognisable today as a BOOLEAN ALGEBRA / LATTICE. He turned Boole's insight into a finished branch of mathematics. LIVE: the Boolean lattice on the subsets of {a,b,c} (8 nodes by inclusion) — click two nodes, see their meet & join; the axioms hold (distributive + absorption verified, a Boolean algebra; self-dual). CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (the definitive synthesis of Boolean algebra & the calculus of relations + duality; the standard reference for decades) / CONSOLIDATOR-NOT-ORIGINATOR (the core ideas are Boole's/Peirce's/Jevons's; his genius was completeness & rigour — and the algebraic tradition he perfected was about to be eclipsed by [[gottlob-frege]]'s quantificational logic) / A-SECOND-CLAIM (the SCHRÖDER–BERNSTEIN theorem in set theory bears his name — his proof had a gap, Bernstein fixed it). ⚑ He perfected the algebra of logic just as Frege overshadowed it for foundations — but the EQUATIONAL view he finished is exactly the one that came roaring back through [[claude-shannon]] into computer science.
